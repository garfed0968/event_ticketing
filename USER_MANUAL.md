# User Manual - Event Ticketing System

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration
1. Click **Register** in the top navigation bar.
2. Select your Role: **Organizer** or **Attender**.
3. Fill in your details and click **Register**.

### Profile Management
- Click **Edit Profile** in the navigation menu.
- You can update your **Username** and **Email**.
- To change your **Password**, enter your old password and the new one.

---

## 2. Guide for Event Organizers

### Creating an Event
1. Log in -> **Dashboard** -> **Create New Event**.
2. Fill in details (Title, Date, Price, etc.).
3. Click **Publish Event**.

### Managing Tickets
1. Go to **Pending Ticket Approvals** on your dashboard.
2. Click **View** to check the payment proof.
3. **Approve (Green Check)** or **Decline (Red X)**.

### Processing Refunds
1. If a user requests a refund, it appears in the **Refund Requests** section.
2. Verify you have manually returned the money to the user.
3. Click **Confirm Refund**. The ticket status changes to "Refunded".

---

## 3. Guide for Attenders

### Buying a Ticket
1. Browse **Upcoming Events** on the home page.
2. Click **Buy Ticket**.
3. Select Payment Method (e.g., CBE) and Upload Proof.
4. Click **Confirm Purchase**.

### Managing Tickets
1. Go to **My Tickets**.
   - **Yellow**: Pending approval.
   - **Green**: Approved & Valid.
   - **Red**: Declined.
   - **Blue**: Refund Requested.
   - **Grey**: Refunded.
2. **Refund**: If your ticket is Approved but you can't go, click **Request Refund**.

### Forgot Password?
1. On the Login page, click **Forgot Password?**.
2. Enter your email.
3. Follow the simulated link to reset your credentials.

---

## 4. Guide for Admins

### Accessing Admin Panel
- Login with the admin credentials (default: `admin@event.com` / `admin123`).
- Click **Admin Panel** in the top navigation.

### Managing Users
- **Dashboard**: View User, Event, and Ticket counts.
- **User List**: See all registered users and their roles.
- **Delete User**: Click the **Delete** button (Trash icon) to remove a user.
  - *Warning*: This deletes all events and tickets associated with that user.
